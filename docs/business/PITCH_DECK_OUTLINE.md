## Slide 2 — Problem

Headline:

- browser-heavy QA is slow, flaky, and expensive to maintain

Bullets:

- Playwright and Cypress pain is real and budgeted
- critical business rules are still verified at the browser layer
- agencies repeat setup across client projects
- cloud primitives do not solve the workflow layer above them

---

## Slide 3 — Solution

Headline:

- move critical verification below the browser

Bullets:

- typed tool contracts for high-value flows
- managed runtime, auth, metering, and governance
- browser retained as a thin smoke and evidence layer
- the same contract works in CI, product flows, CLI, and AI agents
